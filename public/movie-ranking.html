My 3 Favorite Movies

by Giovanna Chirico

Pride and Prejudice

This is a movie based on the same title book by Jane Austen. It tells the love story of Elizabeth Bennet, a very smart and witty girl from a more humble family, and Mr. Darcy, a handsome and somber man from a rich family. This beautiful love story is situated in the countryside of England.

Devil Wears Prada

This movie tells the story of Andy Sachs, an recently graduated journalist that is hired to be the personal assistant of Miranda Priestly, the editor-in-chief of Runway magazine, a fashion magazine. Although with no previous experince in fashion, Andy excells in her role and endures all the excesive demands and humiliating treatment from Miranda, but it takes a toll on her personal life.

Anastasia

This is a movie loosely based on the royal Romanov family of Russia. It tells the story of Anastasia Romanov as if she escaped her death and got separated from her only living close relative, her grandmother, the Dowager Empress Maria Romanov. Anastasia looses all her memories and ends up in an orphanage, however at 18 she gets expelled from the orphanage and must continue with her life.
